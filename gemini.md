Pokémon Flux LoRA Trainer.
This project provides a complete Python pipeline for preparing a dataset, captioning, and fine-tuning a Low-Rank Adaptation (LoRA) on the Flux 2 klein 9b text-to-image to generate high-quality, stylistically accurate Pokémon.

## 💻 Prerequisites & Hardware
* **OS:** Windiws 11
* **GPU:** VRAM >= 32GB
* **Python:** 3.13.x
* **CUDA:** 12.8+
